    What Is GeoBoy?

    GeoBoy is my interactive website which compiles a combination of games, and facts to help you improve your geographic knowledge.


    Introduction to the author


    Noel Pancras is a 14 year old Stonehill student from Bangalore who spends his time doing
    things, he created GeoBoy because he saw a gaping hole in the geographic knowledge of the general
    population of today. To do this, he decided to use a combination of HTML, CSS and JavaScript to make an
    interactive website which he hopes you will enjoy using, and learning from.
